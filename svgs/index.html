Ahmed Abusnaina

Research Assistant at SEAL

University of Central Florida

Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Central Florida in Orlando. I obtained my Bachelor at An-Najah National University, in West Bank, Palestine, in 2018.

At UCF, I am a member of the Security Analytics Research Lab, and advised by Prof. Aziz Mohaisen. I am intested in information security, especially IoT security and Machine Learning.

More about me can be found in my CV.

Research

Machine Learning

Applications of machine and deep learning in security and privacy fields.

Natural Language Processing

Using NLP in data analysis and clustering in different security applications.

Adversarial Machine Learning

Practical attacks on malware detection systems and potential defenses.
